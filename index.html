About Me


Hello World! I'm Narjis, a proud tech enthusiast, coding aficionado and globetrotter who's fascinated with the intricacies of AI.

I'd definitely say my curiosity is the main trait that has driven the progression of my life. I was born in Marseille, France and have lived in Canada, Australia, South Korea, Portugal, United Kingdom and most notably China, where I spent an enlightening 7 years soaking up the culture. Across my travels, I've experienced a lot of different types of work, cultures and disciplines. After returning to Europe after my stint in China, I became a lot more interested in tech, beginning my journey into product management, using the at-home time that the pandemic forced us into to pick up some new skills.

As a polyglot product manager with a background in account management and business development, I've had to don several hats in my professinnal career. These experiences, coupled with my adaptability and knack for embracing new environments, prepared me for to jump into dynamic world of tech. So, here I am, at the intersection of my passion for AI and my expertise in product management, ready to make my mark in a more technical domain.

Road to Code


Embarking on the road to coding has been nothing short of a thrilling expedition for me. It all started with the basics - HTML and CSS. As I started to feel more comfortable with it, I decided to give JavaScript a try, where interactivity became my new (nemesis) best friend. These days I am knee deep Python and trying to build some data science skills.

Throughout this journey, I've realized I am a problem-solver at heart, someone who relishes the challenge of a good Sudoku puzzle and finds beauty in a well-written line of code. So, it's clear to me that software development should be more than just a hobby for me; it's the perfect destination for this journey I'm on.
